Alun Ward started Eye Division in 2005, after ten years in the industry, to build websites for artists. Since then he's been helping artists, arts organisations, businesses, charities and universities and research programmes find their way on the web.

Eye Division provides design and web development expertise, and can call on a network of local suppliers for content writing, photography and video, branding and marketing support.

Alun Ward

Alun still maintains the site of his very first customer twenty years on, and with Eye Division has now developed over 120 websites. Away from Eye Division, he works on art projects.
